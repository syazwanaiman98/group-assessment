History

The Makette Steamboat & Grill restaurant was established by Mr Mohamad Hilmi bin Mohamad Ariffin. with the opening of the first branch in Tanah Merah, Kelantan on 15 December 2016. He opened the first branch with capitalized RM100,000. The first business site is rented at a price of RM2000 per month at Jalan Besar Tanah Merah-Pasir Mas, Tanah Merah, Kelantan

In June 28, 2017, Makette Sdn. Bhd. has been enacted and registered with the Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia (SSM). The name "Makette" has been registered as a trademark with Intellectual Property Corporation of Malaysia (MYIPO) on 26 April 2017. The restaurant is branded as "Makette Steamboat & Grill Restaurant".

The scope of business of Makette Sdn. Bhd. is a restaurant that serves buffet style and Korean style (steamboat and grill) buffet with a variety of seafood, marinated meat, vegetables and beverages. Makette Sdn Bhd has three branch in Kelantan and Terengganu. The first branch at Tanah Merah, Kelantan, second branch at Chendering, Kuala Terengganu and Third at Panji, Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It has Central kitchen as a warehouse where the food is process before sends to the all branch and it has been built on 6 October 2017 to manage all products more systematic and arranged. They have 13 workers in Central Kitchen including chef, chef assistant and general workers and 10 workers at Tanah Merah Branch. Makette has about 37 tables with capacity 200 people at one time.

Operates using basic tools in the beginning such as cutting small chickens using a knife and not using a large machine to process food as it will cost a huge amount beyond his ability to buy the machine at that time. And now, they are now able to buy machines that can increase Makette's productivity such machines for peeling onions, machine for cutting chicken and meat and large blender.
